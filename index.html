Our Mission

A thread of hope connecting people & nature

The Eco Development Division of Kalakad Mundanthurai Tiger Reserve works at the intersection of livelihoods and conservation in one of India's most biodiverse regions.

01

Community Empowerment

Sustainable incomes for families living near the reserve, reducing dependence on forest resources and strengthening local economies.

02

Women's Livelihoods

Hundreds of women earning with dignity through tailoring, embroidery, and certified food enterprises — changing lives from within.

03

Tribal Inclusion

The Kani tribal community is at the heart of our organic produce programme — supported with certifications, training, and direct market access.

04

Forest Conservation

Alternative livelihoods reduce pressure on forests, protecting wildlife habitats and ensuring the Western Ghats remain a living ecosystem.
